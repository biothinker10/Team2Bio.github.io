About our product

Switching to environmentally friendly packaging for takeout would primarily address: SDG 12 - Responsible Consumption and Production. In terms of health, if plastics package a hot temperature food the micro plastic can get into the food and it can cause health problems So, reducing the overall amount of plastic waste that is used in the food packaging takeaways for environmental and health advantages.

About Us

Our Backstory.

By Koh - As someone who always buys takeaway. I feel that single-use plastics used in takeaway packaging and delivery packing go to waste. I always feel guilty throwing away single-use plastics. I feel like plastic is not meant for single use

  • Joining SECIP 2023 program, my team and I have the same thought about the issue of plastic. Our team originally was going to make a prototype of alternative packaging, but we realize that there are many alternatives on the market. It is just that they are too expensive!!!
  • In addition, we have limited time to make a new alternative prototype from scratch. With this, we had an idea we want to make alternative packaging in the market cheaper.
  • Especially, in restaurants and food delivery services. Where customers and restaurant owners do not want to bear the cost of expensive packaging.
